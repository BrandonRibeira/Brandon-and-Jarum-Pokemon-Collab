Type Matchups

Every individual pokemon has up to two "types". these determine their weaknesses, resistances and invulnerabilities, though rare, to other typings.
for instance, fighting types are known to be strong against rock, ice, normal, steel and dark.
this is because those things could be considered especially vulnerable to physical violence.
However, you cannot punch a ghost, so fighting attacks won't deal damage to ghost-types. Further, for less intuitive reasons, it's hard to punch birds, ESPers, bugs, poison and fairies.
i'll let you figure out why. for more questions, you can consult the graph above.
